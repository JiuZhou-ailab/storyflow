# Storyflow Promo Video Design

## Style Prompt

Soft utilitarian desktop product video for Chinese writers. Use real screen recordings as the primary visual asset, with restrained floating captions, low shadows, cool light neutrals, and a blue Storyflow accent. The result should feel like a quiet macOS product walkthrough, not a loud launch trailer.

## Colors

- `#F4F6F8` background wash
- `#111827` primary text and dark CTA
- `#667085` secondary text
- `#2563EB` Storyflow blue accent
- `#FFFFFF` caption surface

## Typography

- Display and Chinese text: `PingFang SC`, `Avenir Next`, system sans-serif
- Utility labels and counters: `SF Mono`, `ui-monospace`, monospace

## Motion

- Medium-energy product explainer pacing
- Push-wipe transitions between scenes
- Caption entrance uses soft y/opacity/scale motion
- No abrupt jump cuts

## What NOT to Do

- No neon gradients, glow-heavy tech styling, or dramatic dark backgrounds
- No generic stock mockups replacing the real product screen
- No dense caption blocks that cover the client UI
- No random decorative cards or repeated icon grids
